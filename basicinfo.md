# Basic Intake Information

Use this form to collect baseline child, caregiver, medical, and consent details before screening. Fields marked "(optional)" may be left blank by caregivers.

## Child Details
- Full name (first, last)
- Date of birth (YYYY-MM-DD)
- Age (years, months) â€” auto-calculated when possible
- Sex / Gender (as reported by caregiver)
- Primary language(s) spoken at home

## Caregiver & Contact
- Primary caregiver name
- Relationship to child (e.g., mother, father, guardian)
- Phone number (best contact)
- Email address

## Medical & Developmental History
- Birth history (term, preterm, NICU, complications)
- Known medical conditions or diagnoses
- Current medications
- Hearing status (any concerns or tests?)
- Vision status (any concerns or tests?)

## Education & Services
- Current setting (home, daycare, preschool, school)
- Grade / class (if applicable)

## Family & Environmental Factors
- Family history of learning difficulties (reading, spelling, dyslexia, ADHD, etc.)
- Household languages and literacy practices (books at home, reading routines)

## Consent & Administrative
- Consent to use screening data: YES / NO 
- Consent date

---

Notes for intake implementers:
- Make `Date of birth` required to calculate age automatically.
- Keep `Primary language(s)` and `Family history` to help interpret results.
- Store consent choices and contact details securely and log consent date/time.
